About Elon Musk

Elon Musk is a South African-born American entrepreneur and businessman known for founding companies such as Tesla, SpaceX, Neuralink, and The Boring Company. He wants to make humans multi-planetary.

Early Career

Elon Musk started with Zip2 and later co-founded X.com which turned into PayPal.

Major Companies

Tesla, Inc.

Electric vehicles, solar energy, EV revolution.

SpaceX

Reusable rockets, Mars mission and space exploration.

Neuralink & The Boring Company

Brain-computer interface & underground tunnel transport.
